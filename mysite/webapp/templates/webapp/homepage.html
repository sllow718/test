Resource Information Database

Envisioning a better future

Recycling in Singapore has always been tough given the lack of structure and community. The Research Information Database(RID) hopes to fill up this gap in Singapore by creating a database that seeks to provide free and direct information to how recycling can create useful byproducts. We hope that organisations will find this a useful tool when they seek for avenues to recycle their products. We currently have databases for food and plastic and are currently looking for contributors who can contribute for other forms of waste.

Accessible to all

The Resource Information Database (RID) was established to encourage recycling in Singapore by allowing public access to the list of companies which provide recycling services to produce materials required for specific uses.

RID is open to academics and recycling companies to input data of outputs and processes with food, paper and plastic wastes. Other categories of wastes are also encouraged to be added on to the database. With RID, companies requiring raw materials are then able to tap onto the RID to decide the optimum process and parameters or company required to obtain these recycled raw materials.
