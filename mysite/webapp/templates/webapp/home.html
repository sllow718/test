FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTION

What is the Resource Information Database(RID)?

RID is known as Resources Information Database that allows you to upload and search available materials on the cloud. RID promotes communication and collaboration across companies to bring the most value out of wastes before going into the landfill

How do I add new resources?

Click the 'plus' icon on the navigation bar and then fill up the form accordingly to add new resources into the database.

How do I edit the existing resources?

Click the 'edit' icon on the navigation bar to retrieve and edit the resource.

How do I retrieve the existing resources?

Click the 'search' icon on the navigation bar to retrieve a resource that is of your interest.
